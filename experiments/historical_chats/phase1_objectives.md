# Phase 1 Objectives

The goals of the first phase of questionnaire analysis were:

1. To understand how to analyze a questionnaire like the one that was administered.
2. To prepare, within a few days, a presentation for the project team with the analysis results.
3. During this phase, it became clear that a proper statistical analysis with appropriate tools was needed, also taking into account possible significant differences across subgroups: students’ gender, school year, and study track.
